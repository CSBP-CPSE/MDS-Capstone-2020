Instructions
  • Scroll in the map to zoom in and out.
  • Click and drag the map to move.
  • Hover over a public health unit or long-term care home (represented as points) to view details.
Last updated: June 24, 2020
Element Description
Incidence Rate Ratio The number of cases for each PHU was determined from data provided by Statistics Canada and divided by the population of the corresponding PHU.
Fatality Ratio \[no. fatalities \over no. cases\]
Amenity Score Amenity richness was calculated from an amenity score provided by Statistics Canada. Each dissemination block receives a score of 0 or 1, representing low or high proximity to amenities, respectively. The mean of dissemination block scores for a single PHU was calculated.


Long-Term Care Home Outbreaks in Ontario

Outbreak status describes whether a long-term care home is currently experiencing a COVID-19 outbreak (active) or not (inactive), as of June 12, 2020.


Data sources: Data from Statistics Canada - proximity data, LTC data, Ontario Covid-19 data and health data.
